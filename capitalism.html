CAPITALISM – Available Now
Steam

'CAPITALISM The action board game for one player' is a real-time board game/ card game hybrid where your goal is to become a millionaire. Build your property empire, compete for cash prizes in high stakes mini-games, and (hopefully) earn more than you spend.

A mix of strategy and action, with minigames spanning a variety of genres. Arcade classics, platforming, bullet hell and more. CAPITALISM also features a character creator that lets you design literally anything you can think of.
